THE GENESIS

Born in the heart of 2026, **Strange World Studio** was established with a singular, dark purpose: to craft digital experiences that don't just entertain, but linger in the back of your mind long after the screen goes black.

We are a small team of rebels. We rejected the polished, predictable nature of corporate horror to return to the roots of the genre: **Atmosphere, Vulnerability, and the Unknown.**

ADAPTIVE AI

Our entities don't just follow scripts. They react to your movement and panic levels.

THE PHILOSOPHY

At Strange World, we believe horror is a conversation between the developer and the player's subconscious. Our "Void Engine" is designed to be unpredictable.

When you play a Strange World title, you aren't just playing a game—you are entering a simulated nightmare.
